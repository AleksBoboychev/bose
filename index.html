Life without music, would be a mistake.

BÖSE is a Bulgarian metal band formed in Southampton, Hampshire on the 25/6/2021. BÖSE started recording their debut album on the 18th of April 2022 and finished it on the 25/5/2022. BÖSE released their second album "Profitable Wars" on the 6/7/2022 talking about the economical state of the world while wars are going on, enduring pain on the battlefield and going through the inevitable. BÖSE'S started recording their 3rd album called Apocalyptic World, the genereal theme of the album is society and the worshippers of the system that we are all enslaved to one way or another.
